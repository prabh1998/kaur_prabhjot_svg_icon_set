DRINKS & SWEETS

BREAD

Bread is a staple food prepared from a dough of flour and water, usually by baking.

CAKE

Cakes can also be filled with fruit preserves, nuts or dessert sauces, iced with buttercream or other icings, and decorated with marzipan, piped borders, or candied fruit.

CHOCOLATE

Chocolate is a food made from the seeds of a cacao bean.

Coffee

A strong, concentrated coffee made by forcing pressurized water through finely ground coffee beans.

CONE

Cone is usually made of a wafer similar in texture to a waffle, which enables ice cream to be held in the hand and eaten without a bowl or spoon.

COOKIE

A cookie is a baked or cooked food that is small, flat and sweet

HONEY

A small round container that often has a handle and that is used for drinking liquids.

DONUT

A donut is a round, deep fried cake, usually with a hole in the center.

Kettle

Kettle is a type of pot, specialized for boiling water, with a lid, spout, and handle.
